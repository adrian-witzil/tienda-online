What's this all about?

Tienda Online is exactly what the name suggests. We are taking what you’ve grown up with, when your mom sent you to the tiny corner store and combining it with the ease and convenience of online shopping. Tienda Online is an app that can be downloaded on any mobile divide or you can just go to our website. Utilizing a very familiar ecommerce layout, you can add items to your cart and check out. You will notice that the items available are consistent with what you would find in any tienda down the street. Upon checkout, your items will be hand picked by our caring staff to make sure the items are fresh and to your liking. Then given to a friendly and courteous delivery person to take to your door within an hour after checkout. Payment options are flexible for those that don’t have a credit card or bank account. Cash payment upon arrival will always be an option. Tienda Online is currently only available to residents of Corozal Town, Belize but will shortly be expanding into the rest of the country and beyond.
